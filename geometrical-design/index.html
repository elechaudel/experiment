The three graces — Theo Van Doesburg, 1917

Theo Van Doesburg is a Dutch artist who co-founded De Stijl, an artistic magazine promoting Neoplasticism, with related artists in 1917. More than a magazine De Stijl is also a movement in reaction to its opposite counterpart at that time: the Modern Baroque, an Expressionist movement. Artists from the De Stijl movement were favouring abstraction using simple shapes and primary colors over distorted shapes and ornementations. Rectangular forms, asymetry, opposition, straight lines, ... are the basic elements of every Neoplastic Artwork.
